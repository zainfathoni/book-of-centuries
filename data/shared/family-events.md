# Family Shared Historical Events

## Events Important to Whole Family

### Religious History
- 30 AD **Jesus Christ** Crucifixion and resurrection in Jerusalem #worldhistory [family]
- 325 AD **Council of Nicea** Establishes fundamental Christian doctrine #worldhistory [family]

### Major Historical Transitions  
- 476 AD **Fall of Western Roman Empire** End of ancient world, beginning of medieval period #worldhistory [family]
- 1453 AD **Fall of Constantinople** End of Byzantine Empire, end of Middle Ages #worldhistory [family]

### Discoveries and Inventions
- ~1440 AD **Gutenberg Printing Press** Revolutionizes learning and knowledge #discoveries [family]
- 1492 AD **Columbus Reaches Americas** European discovery of New World #discoveries [family]

## Notes
These are historical events that are significant to our family's understanding of world history and will appear in all children's individual books.
