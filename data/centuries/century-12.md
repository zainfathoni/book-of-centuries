# Century 12 (1101-1200 AD)

## Major Historical Events and Figures

- 1122 AD **Concordat of Worms** Resolves Investiture Controversy between Pope and Emperor #politics [family]
- 1144 AD **Second Crusade** Response to fall of Edessa, led by Louis VII and Conrad III #wars [family]
- 1154 AD **Henry II of England** Begins Plantagenet dynasty, controls vast territories in France #rulers [family]
- 1170 AD **Murder of Thomas Becket** Archbishop killed at Canterbury, becomes martyr #worldhistory [family]
- 1171 AD **Saladin** Becomes ruler of Egypt and Syria, unites Muslim forces #rulers [family]
- 1187 AD **Battle of Hattin** Saladin defeats Crusaders, recaptures Jerusalem #wars [family]
- 1189 AD **Third Crusade** Richard the Lionheart, Philip Augustus, Frederick Barbarossa #wars [family]
- 1192 AD **Treaty of Jaffa** Ends Third Crusade, allows Christian pilgrimage to Jerusalem #politics [family]

## Key Themes of This Century
- High point of Crusading movement
- Rise of Saladin and Muslim reconquest
- Development of Gothic architecture
- Growth of universities and scholasticism
- Expansion of trade and commerce
- Strengthening of royal power in England and France
