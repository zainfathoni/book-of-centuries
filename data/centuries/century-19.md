# Century 19 (1801-1900 AD)

## Major Historical Events and Figures

- 1804 AD **Napoleon Crowned Emperor** French Empire established, Continental System begins #rulers [family]
- 1815 AD **Battle of Waterloo** Napoleon defeated, Congress of Vienna restores European balance #wars [family]
- 1821 AD **Greek War of Independence** Ottoman Empire loses control of Greece #politics [family]
- 1848 AD **Year of Revolutions** Democratic uprisings across Europe challenge old order #politics [family]
- 1861-1865 AD **American Civil War** Union preserves nation, abolishes slavery #wars [family]
- 1869 AD **Suez Canal Opens** Links Mediterranean and Red Sea, transforms global trade #discoveries [family]
- 1871 AD **German Unification** Prussia creates German Empire under Kaiser Wilhelm I #politics [family]
- 1885 AD **Berlin Conference** European powers partition Africa among colonial empires #politics [family]

## Key Themes of This Century
- Nationalism and democratic revolutions
- Industrial Revolution transforms society
- Abolition of slavery and serfdom
- European colonialism peaks globally
- Railroad and telegraph connect the world
- Scientific progress in evolution and medicine
