# Century 20 (1901-2000 AD)

## Major Historical Events and Figures

- 1914-1918 AD **World War I** "The Great War" devastates Europe, ends empires #wars [family]
- 1917 AD **Russian Revolution** Lenin leads Bolsheviks to power, creates Soviet Union #politics [family]
- 1929 AD **Great Depression** Global economic collapse affects millions worldwide #disasters [family]
- 1933-1945 AD **Adolf Hitler** Nazi dictator leads Germany into World War II #rulers [family]
- 1939-1945 AD **World War II** Deadliest conflict in human history, Holocaust occurs #wars [family]
- 1945 AD **Atomic Bombs** Hiroshima and Nagasaki end WWII, nuclear age begins #discoveries [family]
- 1969 AD **Moon Landing** Neil Armstrong first human to walk on the moon #discoveries [family]
- 1989 AD **Fall of Berlin Wall** Symbol of Cold War division, leads to German reunification #politics [family]
- 1991 AD **End of Soviet Union** Cold War ends, world becomes unipolar under US leadership #politics [family]

## Key Themes of This Century
- Two devastating world wars reshape global order
- Rise and fall of totalitarian regimes
- Nuclear weapons change nature of warfare
- Decolonization creates many new nations
- Technological revolution transforms daily life
- Cold War divides world into competing ideologies
