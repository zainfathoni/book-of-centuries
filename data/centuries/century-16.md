# Century 16 (1501-1600 AD)

## Major Historical Events and Figures

- 1517 AD **Martin Luther's 95 Theses** Begins Protestant Reformation #worldhistory [family]
- 1519-1521 AD **Conquest of Aztec Empire** Hernán Cortés defeats Montezuma II #discoveries [family]
- 1532 AD **Conquest of Inca Empire** Francisco Pizarro captures Atahualpa #discoveries [family]
- 1534 AD **Act of Supremacy** Henry VIII breaks with Rome, establishes Church of England #worldhistory [family]
- 1545-1563 AD **Council of Trent** Catholic Counter-Reformation reforms church practices #worldhistory [family]
- 1571 AD **Battle of Lepanto** Holy League defeats Ottoman navy in Mediterranean #wars [family]
- 1588 AD **Spanish Armada** England defeats Spanish invasion fleet #wars [family]
- 1598 AD **Edict of Nantes** Henry IV grants religious tolerance to French Huguenots #politics [family]

## Key Themes of This Century
- Protestant Reformation splits Western Christianity
- Spanish conquest of American empires
- Age of Exploration and global trade expansion
- Scientific Revolution begins with Copernicus
- Ottoman Empire reaches greatest extent
- Renaissance art and culture flourish
