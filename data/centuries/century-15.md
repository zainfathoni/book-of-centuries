# Century 15 (1401-1500 AD)

## Major Historical Events and Figures

- 1415 AD **Battle of Agincourt** Henry V's famous victory over French in Hundred Years' War #wars [family]
- 1431 AD **Joan of Arc** Executed by English after leading French resistance #rulers [family]
- 1440s AD **Johannes Gutenberg** Invents printing press, revolutionizes knowledge #discoveries [family]
- 1453 AD **Fall of Constantinople** Ottoman Empire conquers last Byzantine stronghold #wars [family]
- 1469 AD **Marriage of Isabella and Ferdinand** Unifies Spain under Catholic Monarchs #rulers [family]
- 1485 AD **Battle of Bosworth Field** Henry VII begins Tudor dynasty in England #wars [family]
- 1492 AD **Christopher Columbus** Discovers Americas, begins Age of Exploration #discoveries [family]
- 1498 AD **Vasco da Gama** First European to reach India by sea route #discoveries [family]

## Key Themes of This Century
- Renaissance humanism and artistic achievement
- Age of Exploration and discovery of New World
- Fall of Byzantine Empire to Ottoman Turks
- Invention of printing press spreads literacy
- Unification of Spain and expansion of Christian kingdoms
- Beginning of global trade and colonial empires
