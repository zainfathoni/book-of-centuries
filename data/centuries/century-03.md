# Century 3 (201-300 AD)

## Major Historical Events and Figures

- 211-217 AD **Caracalla** Roman Emperor grants citizenship to all free inhabitants #rulers [family]
- 235-284 AD **Crisis of the Third Century** Period of near-constant civil war in Rome #politics [family]
- 249-251 AD **Decius** First emperor to systematically persecute Christians empire-wide #rulers [family]
- 260 AD **Valerian** Roman Emperor captured by Persian King Shapur I #wars [family]
- 270-275 AD **Aurelian** "Restorer of the World" reunifies Roman Empire #rulers [family]
- 284-305 AD **Diocletian** Divides empire into tetrarchy, last great persecution #rulers [family]
- 293 AD **Tetrarchy Established** Empire divided among four rulers for stability #politics [family]
- 303-311 AD **Great Persecution** Diocletian's systematic attempt to destroy Christianity #worldhistory [family]

## Key Themes of This Century
- Crisis and near-collapse of Roman Empire
- Rise of military emperors and civil wars
- Systematic persecution of Christians
- Economic inflation and military pressures
- Administrative reforms and division of empire
- Growing influence of Christianity despite persecution
