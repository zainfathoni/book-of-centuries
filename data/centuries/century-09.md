## Key Themes of This Century
- Carolingian Empire reaches peak then fragments
- Viking Age expansion across Europe and beyond
- Development of feudalism in Western Europe
- Alfred the Great's resistance to Viking conquest
- Foundation of early Russian state
- Decline of central authority, rise of local lordship
