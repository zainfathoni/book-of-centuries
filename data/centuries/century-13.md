# Century 13 (1201-1300 AD)

## Major Historical Events and Figures

- 1204 AD **Fourth Crusade** Crusaders sack Constantinople instead of Holy Land #wars [family]
- 1215 AD **Magna Carta** King John forced to limit royal power in England #politics [family]
- 1217 AD **Fifth Crusade** Attempts to conquer Egypt, ends in failure #wars [family]
- 1227 AD **Death of Genghis Khan** Mongol Empire at its peak expansion #rulers [family]
- 1241 AD **Battle of Mohi** Mongols devastate Hungary in European campaign #wars [family]
- 1258 AD **Fall of Baghdad** Mongols destroy Abbasid Caliphate, end Islamic Golden Age #wars [family]
- 1270 AD **Eighth Crusade** Louis IX dies in Tunisia, last major Crusade #wars [family]
- 1291 AD **Fall of Acre** Mamluks capture last Crusader stronghold in Holy Land #wars [family]

## Key Themes of This Century
- Mongol conquests reshape Eurasia
- End of Crusading era in the Holy Land
- Rise of universities and scholastic thought
- Gothic architecture reaches maturity
- Magna Carta establishes constitutional principles
- Growth of trade between East and West
