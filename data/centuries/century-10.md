# Century 10 (901-1000 AD)

## Major Historical Events and Figures

- 911 AD **Treaty of Saint-Clair-sur-Epte** Normandy established as Viking duchy #politics [family]
- 955 AD **Battle of Lechfeld** Otto I defeats Magyars, ends their raids #wars [family]
- 962 AD **Otto I Crowned Emperor** Holy Roman Empire formally established #rulers [family]
- 969 AD **Fatimid Conquest of Egypt** Shi'ite dynasty takes control of Egypt #wars [family]
- 980-1037 AD **Ibn Sina (Avicenna)** Persian polymath advances medicine and philosophy #discoveries [family]
- 987 AD **Hugh Capet** Begins Capetian dynasty in France #rulers [family]
- 988 AD **Conversion of Vladimir I** Russia adopts Orthodox Christianity #worldhistory [family]
- 1000 AD **Leif Erikson** Vikings reach North America, 500 years before Columbus #discoveries [family]

## Key Themes of This Century
- End of Viking raids and their settlement in Europe
- Establishment of Holy Roman Empire under Otto I
- Religious conversion of Eastern Europe to Christianity
- Rise of new European kingdoms and dynasties
- Continued Islamic Golden Age in science and philosophy
- Early exploration reaching the New World
