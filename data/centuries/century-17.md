# Century 17 (1601-1700 AD)

## Major Historical Events and Figures

- 1618-1648 AD **Thirty Years' War** Religious conflicts devastate Central Europe #wars [family]
- 1620 AD **Mayflower Compact** Pilgrims establish Plymouth Colony in North America #discoveries [family]
- 1642-1651 AD **English Civil War** Parliament defeats King Charles I, establishes Commonwealth #politics [family]
- 1648 AD **Peace of Westphalia** Ends Thirty Years' War, establishes modern state system #politics [family]
- 1649 AD **Execution of Charles I** England becomes a republic under Oliver Cromwell #rulers [family]
- 1660 AD **Stuart Restoration** Charles II returns to English throne #rulers [family]
- 1683 AD **Siege of Vienna** Polish-Austrian forces defeat Ottoman expansion #wars [family]
- 1689 AD **Glorious Revolution** William and Mary take English throne, constitutional monarchy #politics [family]

## Key Themes of This Century
- Religious wars and rise of tolerance
- Scientific Revolution with Newton and Galileo
- Absolutism peaks under Louis XIV
- English constitutional development
- Colonial expansion in Americas
- Beginning of Enlightenment thinking
