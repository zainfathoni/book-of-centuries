# Century 11 (1001-1100 AD)

## Major Historical Events and Figures

- 1016 AD **Canute the Great** Becomes king of England, Denmark, and Norway #rulers [family]
- 1054 AD **Great Schism** Eastern Orthodox and Roman Catholic churches split #worldhistory [family]
- 1066 AD **Battle of Hastings** William the Conqueror defeats Harold II, Norman conquest of England #wars [family]
- 1071 AD **Battle of Manzikert** Seljuk Turks defeat Byzantines, opens Anatolia to Turkish settlement #wars [family]
- 1076 AD **Investiture Controversy** Conflict between Pope Gregory VII and Holy Roman Emperor #politics [family]
- 1086 AD **Domesday Book** William I orders comprehensive survey of England #discoveries [family]
- 1095 AD **First Crusade Called** Pope Urban II launches holy war to reclaim Jerusalem #wars [family]
- 1099 AD **Capture of Jerusalem** Crusaders establish Latin Kingdom of Jerusalem #wars [family]

## Key Themes of This Century
- Norman conquest transforms England and European politics
- Great Schism permanently divides Christianity
- Beginning of the Crusades reshapes medieval world
- Seljuk Turkish expansion challenges Byzantine Empire
- Investiture Controversy defines church-state relations
- Rise of feudalism across Western Europe
