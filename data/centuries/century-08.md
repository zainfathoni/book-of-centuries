# Century 8 (701-800 AD)

## Major Historical Events and Figures

- 711 AD **Islamic Conquest of Iberia** Tariq ibn Ziyad crosses from Morocco to Spain #wars [family]
- 717-718 AD **Siege of Constantinople** Islamic expansion stopped at Byzantine capital #wars [family]
- 732 AD **Battle of Tours** Charles Martel stops Muslim advance into France #wars [family]
- 741-768 AD **Pepin the Short** First Carolingian king, father of Charlemagne #rulers [family]
- 750 AD **Abbasid Revolution** New dynasty takes over Islamic caliphate #politics [family]
- 756 AD **Donation of Pepin** Creates Papal States in central Italy #politics [family]
- 762 AD **Baghdad Founded** Abbasid Caliph Al-Mansur builds new capital #construction [family]
- 786-809 AD **Harun al-Rashid** Golden age of Abbasid caliphate and Islamic learning #rulers [family]

## Key Themes of This Century
- High point of Islamic expansion in West and consolidation
- Beginning of Carolingian Renaissance in Western Europe
- Establishment of Papal States and temporal papal power
- Foundation of Baghdad as center of Islamic civilization
- Development of Islamic Golden Age scholarship
- Stabilization of European-Islamic frontier
