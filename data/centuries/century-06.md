# Century 6 (501-600 AD)

## Major Historical Events and Figures

- 527-565 AD **Justinian I** Byzantine Emperor reconquers much of Western Empire #rulers [family]
- 532 AD **Nika Riots** Justinian nearly loses throne in Constantinople uprising #politics [family]
- 533-534 AD **Vandal War** Belisarius reconquers North Africa for Byzantium #wars [family]
- 535-554 AD **Gothic War** Justinian reconquers Italy from Ostrogoths #wars [family]
- 541-549 AD **Plague of Justinian** Bubonic plague devastates Byzantine Empire #disasters [family]
- 570 AD **Muhammad** Prophet of Islam born in Mecca #worldhistory [family]
- 590-604 AD **Pope Gregory I** "Gregory the Great" reforms papacy and mission work #worldhistory [family]
- 597 AD **Augustine of Canterbury** Begins conversion of Anglo-Saxons to Christianity #worldhistory [family]

## Key Themes of This Century
- Justinian's attempt to restore Roman Empire
- Rise of Byzantine power in the East
- Devastating plague reduces population significantly
- Birth of Islam's founder Muhammad
- Continued Christianization of Western Europe
- Consolidation of papal authority under Gregory the Great
