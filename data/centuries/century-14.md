# Century 14 (1301-1400 AD)

## Major Historical Events and Figures

- 1307 AD **Suppression of Knights Templar** Philip IV of France destroys powerful military order #politics [family]
- 1337 AD **Hundred Years' War Begins** England and France enter prolonged conflict #wars [family]
- 1347-1351 AD **Black Death** Bubonic plague kills one-third of Europe's population #worldhistory [family]
- 1356 AD **Battle of Poitiers** English longbowmen defeat French knights #wars [family]
- 1368 AD **Ming Dynasty Founded** Hongwu Emperor expels Mongols from China #rulers [family]
- 1378 AD **Western Schism** Multiple rival popes claim authority #worldhistory [family]
- 1381 AD **Peasants' Revolt** English uprising against poll tax and serfdom #politics [family]
- 1389 AD **Battle of Kosovo** Ottoman Turks defeat Serbian forces, expand into Europe #wars [family]

## Key Themes of This Century
- Black Death transforms European society
- Crisis of medieval institutions and authority
- Rise of Ottoman Empire in southeastern Europe
- Hundred Years' War reshapes medieval warfare
- Ming Dynasty restores Chinese rule
- Beginning of Renaissance humanism in Italy
