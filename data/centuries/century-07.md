# Century 7 (601-700 AD)

## Major Historical Events and Figures

- 610 AD **Heraclius** Byzantine Emperor begins reforms and military reorganization #rulers [family]
- 622 AD **The Hijra** Muhammad migrates from Mecca to Medina, Islamic calendar begins #worldhistory [family]
- 632 AD **Death of Muhammad** Islamic expansion begins under first Caliphs #worldhistory [family]
- 636 AD **Battle of Yarmuk** Muslims defeat Byzantines, conquer Syria and Palestine #wars [family]
- 641 AD **Conquest of Egypt** Arabs take Alexandria, end Byzantine rule in Egypt #wars [family]
- 664 AD **Synod of Whitby** English church chooses Roman over Celtic practices #worldhistory [family]
- 680 AD **Battle of Karbala** Husayn killed, creates Sunni-Shia split in Islam #wars [family]
- 698 AD **Conquest of Carthage** Muslims complete conquest of North Africa #wars [family]

## Key Themes of This Century
- Rapid expansion of Islamic empire
- Decline of Byzantine power in Middle East and Africa
- Beginning of the "Dark Ages" in Western Europe
- Christianization of Anglo-Saxon England
- Development of Islamic theology and law
- End of classical antiquity in many regions
