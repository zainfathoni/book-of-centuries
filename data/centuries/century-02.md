# Century 2 (101-200 AD)

## Major Historical Events and Figures

- 98-117 AD **Trajan** Roman Emperor expands empire to greatest extent #rulers [family]
- 101-106 AD **Dacian Wars** Trajan conquers Dacia (modern Romania) #wars [family]
- 113-117 AD **Trajan's Parthian Campaign** Eastern expansion of Roman Empire #wars [family]
- 117-138 AD **Hadrian** Roman Emperor known for defensive policies #rulers [family]
- 122 AD **Hadrian's Wall** Built across northern England to defend against Scots #construction [family]
- 132-135 AD **Bar Kokhba Revolt** Final Jewish rebellion against Rome #wars [family]
- 138-161 AD **Antoninus Pius** Peaceful reign, extends Hadrian's Wall northward #rulers [family]
- 161-180 AD **Marcus Aurelius** Philosopher emperor, writes Meditations #rulers [family]
- 165-180 AD **Antonine Plague** Pandemic devastates Roman Empire #disasters [family]
- 167-180 AD **Marcomannic Wars** Germanic tribes pressure Roman frontiers #wars [family]
- 180-192 AD **Commodus** Last emperor of Antonine dynasty, gladiator emperor #rulers [family]

## Key Themes of This Century
- Height of Roman territorial expansion under Trajan
- Defensive consolidation under Hadrian
- Golden age of Roman culture and philosophy
- Early Christian church growth despite persecution
- Germanic pressure on Roman frontiers
- Plague and demographic challenges
